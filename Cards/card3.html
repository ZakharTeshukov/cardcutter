W1: Missile Deterrence Eliminates Need for US Military Presence
Belleau, 24

MLA Citation: Belleau, Finnegan. “What is Satan II, the 'world's deadliest' nuclear weapon being tested in Russia?” AP/Russian Defense Ministry Press Service, 4 Oct. 2024, https://www.apnews.com/satan-ii-nuclear-weapon-test.

The RS-28 Sarmat missile, described as "the world’s deadliest weapon," possesses capabilities to carry multiple nuclear warheads and has a range of at least 18,000 km, making it a formidable deterrent. According to Matt Korda, an associate senior researcher at SIPRI, the RS-28 is intended to "discourage nuclear and military escalation" from adversaries like the United States. The extensive destructive potential of such missile systems can deter aggression effectively, reducing the necessity for a continuous US military presence in regions like Taiwan.
